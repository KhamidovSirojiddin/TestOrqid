About LA Zak

For those determined to fly, having no wings is just a little detail.

LA Zak primarily focuses on aerial yoga. Aerial yoga enables you to practice traditional yoga poses with aerial adaptations. These adaptions often give you more flexibility, strength and focus than traditional yoga as you have the benefit of a prop to support you. LA Zak is located in Windsor, Ontario in historic Walkerville at Breathe Pilates and Fitness.
